G2 Esports


G2 Esports, formerly Gamers2 - Spanish esports organization based in Berlin. It consists of sections competing in: League of Legends, Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Rocket League, Fortnite Battle Royale, Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege, iRacing and Valorant. The organization was founded in February 2014 as Gamers2, and in 2015 was renamed G2 Esports.

On September 11, 2015, G2 Esports took over the Team Kinguin roster. They took 3rd place at the DreamHack Open: Cluj-Napoca 2015. In January 2016 the team was taken over again, this time by FaZe Clan. On February 1, 2016, G2 formed a French-speaking team made up of former Titan players. On April 9, Alexandre "bodyy" Pianaro will appear on the team after finishing 9-10 in the MLG Major Championship: Columbus. G2 finished their third ESL Pro League season in second place, losing 2-3 to Luminosity Gaming in the final. Soon after, they retaliated by defeating Luminosity in the Esports Championship Series Season One Finals. On February 3, 2017, three players from Team EnVyUs, Kenny "kennyS" Schrub, Dan "apEX" Madesclaire and Nathan "NBK" Schmitt, joined G2 Esports. They formed the French Super Team. The players who were replaced were Adil "ScreaM" Benrlitom and Cédric "RpK" Guipouy who joined Team EnVyUs and Edouard "SmithZz" Dubourdeaux who became the G2 coach.

Ninjas in Pyjamas



Ninjas in Pajamas (abbreviation: NiP) - Swedish e-sports team founded in 2000 by Tommy Ingermarsson and Emil Christensen. The seat of the team was in Stockholm. The band was disbanded at the end of 2007, and was reactivated in August 2012. Ninjas in Pajamas was one of the founding members of G7 Teams
